# Joja

## Apps

Popular mobile apps for organising events on mobile devices. You can read out the data right in the field and much more.

### SI-Droid Event

<img src="/img/logos/sidroid.png" alt="Si Droid logo" width="150" />

**Operating system**: Android

<a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.joja.sidroid.event.full"><img src="/img/badges/google-play-get-it-on.svg" alt="Google Play download badge" width="180" /></a>

Mobile app for organising small events on your mobile device.

### O-Pal

<img src="/img/logos/opal.png" alt="O-Pal logo" width="150" />

**Operating system**: Android

<a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.joja.opal.event"><img src="/img/badges/google-play-get-it-on.svg" alt="Google Play download badge" width="180" /></a>

## SI-Droid Connector

<img src="/img/logos/connector.png" alt="Connector logo" width="150" />

**Operating system**: Android

<a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.orienteerfeed.ofeed_sidroid_connector"><img src="/img/badges/google-play-get-it-on.svg" alt="Google Play download badge" width="180" /></a>

A mobile app published as an open-source [project](https://github.com/orienteerfeed/ofeed-sidroid-connector) that handles XML uploads to the OFeed platform using the `Results Service` running in `SI-Droid Event` or `O-Pal`. This allows the readout process of your event to be managed easily on your mobile device.
